Lift Logs Privacy Policy

Some information about how we collect no data

What Information Do We Collect?

Lift Logs stores all of your data on your device and none on our servers. This means that we have absolutly no access to any of your information.

What Apps Do We Integrate With?

Apple Health

Users can optionally link their Apple Health account in order to track their Lift Logs-managed workouts in Apple Health.
